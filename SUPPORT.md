# Support

Thank you for using NexusAgent! We're happy to help you get the most out of our software.

## Getting Help

If you encounter any issues or have questions, please follow these steps:

1. **Check the Documentation**: Review the [documentation](../docs) for troubleshooting guides and FAQs.
2. **Search Existing Issues**: Look through our [issue tracker](https://github.com/your-repo/nexusagent/issues) to see if your question has already been addressed.
3. **Contact Support**: If you still need help, reach out to us using one of the methods below.

## Contact Information

- **Email**: support@nexusagent.com
- **Issue Tracker**: https://github.com/your-repo/nexusagent/issues
- **Discord Community**: https://discord.gg/nexusagent
- **Twitter**: @nexusagent

## Reporting Bugs

When reporting a bug, please include:
- A clear and descriptive title
- Steps to reproduce the issue
- Expected behavior vs. actual behavior
- Screenshots or logs, if applicable
- Your environment details (OS, NexusAgent version, etc.)

## Feature Requests

We welcome feature requests! Please:
- Use the issue tracker and label your request as "feature"
- Provide a detailed description of the feature and its benefits
- If possible, include use cases or examples

## Security Concerns

For security-related issues, please email security@nexusagent.com directly. Do not disclose security vulnerabilities in public forums.

## Response Times

We aim to respond to all inquiries within:
- Email: 1-2 business days
- Issue Tracker: 1-3 business days
- Discord: Community support during active hours (typically quicker)

Thank you for contributing to the NexusAgent community!
